Web Location

Looking over Ericssons’s Labs Website I had seen a quite interesting api of theirs named WebLocation. What it does is that it can figure out the location of the users of mobile phones that are on your website. A “must” feature for Social Networking sites like Twitter or Facebook. Unfortunately the feature is only available in Sweden and Norway. I tried to find something that will do the same thing, but I had no luck so far. Maybe they’ll come to Romania eventually, but from what they say the next countries in which the API will be available will be Finland and Germany.

6-cell Battery vs 4 Hour Blackout = 1-0 for the Battery

Last night it was a 4 hour blackout. Luckily a couple of weeks ago I had bought a ACER Aspire 1410 (the 11 inch model) with a 6-cell battery which lasts up to 5-6h on Windows. On Linux I think the performance would be greater but Ubuntu sadly announced me that CPU Scaling is not supported for the Celeron M 743, this is not the only problem I had with Ubuntu on this laptop, configuring the wireless card appeared to be a trial&error process, which tooke me through 4-5 different kernels & a lot of driver installs. But the joy that everything around me was “dead” and I still had my laptop up&running after 4h, it was wonderful.
